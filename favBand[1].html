Zac Brown Band

I am a big country music fan, so Zac Brown Band has always been one of my favorite bands to listen to. One of my good friends is having a birthday party soon and we are all going to see Zac Brown Band perform live! Some of my favorite songs of theirs' are "Whatever it is" and "Knee Deep". My favorite member is this band is Clay Cook. Not only does he have a great voice, but he also writes most of their songs.
