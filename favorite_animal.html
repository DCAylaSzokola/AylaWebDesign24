My Favorite Animal

A favorite animal of mine is a dog. Most dogs are super loyal and loving. Dogs are also very intelligent creatures which makes them great house pets. A Dog is a Human's Best Friend. Some can be very loving and are friendly; while others are naturally wild and outgoing.

Interesting Facts

Suprisingly, a third of households around the world have a dog. These playful, friendly, loyal animals make great companions, but they can also be fierce and tough protectors, or intelligent helpers. another fun fact is that They evolved from a now-extinct species of wolf. Dogs were the first animal trained by humans, which was well over 20,000 years ago. As they evolved from the wolf species, their paws, skulls and teeth shrank, thus becoming more docile and obedient.

Service Dogs

Many dogs are trained to work as guide dogs, which can benefit blind people so that they get around safely. Others are assistance dogs, who keep their owners safe. Lastly, some brave dogs are search and rescue dogs, who help human rescuers save people from danger.
